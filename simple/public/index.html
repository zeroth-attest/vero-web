Verify someone by asking them to prove they control their accounts in real time

Ask someone to sign in to accounts you choose (like Google or LinkedIn) and read a short passphrase out loud to confirm it's really them.

How it works

1. Choose which accounts you want them to verify (e.g. Google, LinkedIn)

2. Send them the verification link

3. They sign in and receive a 3-word passphrase

4. Ask them to read the words out loud — if they match, you know it's really them

Currently supported accounts

Google LinkedIn GitHub Microsoft Apple Discord YouTube SMS Email
