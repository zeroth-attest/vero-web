Vero Voice

Challenge someone with a passphrase tied to one (or several) accounts

Confirm someone is who they say they are using their Google, LinkedIn, GitHub, Microsoft, Facebook, or Apple identity.

How it works

1. You enter the username(s) that you know for the person you wish to verify and pick three secret words

2. Ask them to visit this site and log in with the identity you requested

3. If they can login your secret words appear on their screen

4. Ask them to read the words aloud — if they match, you know it's really them

Supported identity providers

Google LinkedIn GitHub Microsoft Facebook Apple
