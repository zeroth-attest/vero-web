Simple Trust

Verify anyone, right now

Confirm someone is who they say they are using their LinkedIn or Google identity. No app install, no account needed.

How it works

1. You enter who you want to verify and pick 5 secret words

2. Ask them to visit this site and sign in with LinkedIn or Google

3. The same 5 words appear on both screens

4. Ask them to read the words aloud — if they match, you know it's really them
