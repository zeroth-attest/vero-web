Vero Video

Verify someone is real with a unique "light" code that only you can know

An optical one-time biometric that confirms the person you are seeing in a video is the person they claim to be.

How it works

1. You enter the username(s) that you know for the person you wish to verify and generate a unique session key

2. The person opens the Vero app, which flashes a unique light sequence on their phone

3. They hold the phone near their face during the video call

4. The verifier's browser extension confirms the light sequence matches — proving the person is real and present
